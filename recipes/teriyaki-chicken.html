Easy Midweek Teriyaki Chicken

Weekday meal prep just got easier with this chicken teriyaki stir-fry! It's quick, easy, and full of flavor - the perfect meal to power you through your busy week.

Ingredients

For 4 servings

  • 3 chicken breasts, cubed
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup soy sauce(118 mL)
  • ⅓ cup honey(113 g)
  • 1 ½ tablespoons sesame seed, more to garnish
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 small bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli(500 g)
  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced
  • white rice, cooked
